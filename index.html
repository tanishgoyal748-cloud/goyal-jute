About Us

Goyal PP Wovens is a leading manufacturer and exporter of polypropylene (PP) woven fabric and bags. For decades we've supplied durable packaging and industrial textile solutions worldwide.

Our high-capacity production lines, quality control, and custom finishing options ensure each product meets industrial standards and customer needs.
